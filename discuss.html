Bowdoin College Museum of Art: Discussion Board

Welcome to the Discussion Board!

Feel free to search topics and artworks to discuss with fellow Bowdoin students and faculty. No account? No worries! You don't need to make an account to see the discussion. So you'll be able to see conversations and see the engaging discussion students, faculty, and museum staff are having. If you would like to make comments, we encourage you to make an account! With an account, you can customize your profile, message other students and staff, as well as participate in the discussion board! Scroll down to make an account, and get started with us today!

"It's okay to be loud in here."

—Co-Director Anne Collins Goodyear on our open, accessible museum.
Check out our featured discussions over selected artwork from past, current, and upcoming exhibitions below.

Also, feel free to make an account with us. Membership includes exclusive emails to our newsletter and an easier way to participate in the discussion board (add friends, message others, comment with an account). Fill out the simple form on the right.
