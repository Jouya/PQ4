Bowdoin College Museum of Art: Discussion Board

Welcome to the Discussion Board!

Feel free to search topics and artworks to discuss with fellow Bowdoin students and faculty. You don't need to make an account to see the discussion. Every comment you make will be shown in the discussion threads below, and you can leave your name when you comment.

"It's okay to be loud in here."

—Co-Director Anne Collins Goodyear on our open, accessible museum.
